Bo-Bot

With its body made of various recycled objects, this is the first iteration of Bo-Bot, a robot which stemmed from my wish to lighten the workload during rushes at the boba store I worked at.

In the early planning phases, I knew that I wanted Bo-Bot to not only roam the store, but explicitly approach customers. The method of approaching customers is one that gave me some trouble, but it was seeing the Termovision from the movie Terminator, particularly the way the Terminators use thermal vision to find targets, that inspired me to use thermal sensors. By detecting an array of tiles that within an average range of human body temperatures, Bo-Bot knew to lock on to that area as there was likely a human there. Here is a video of Bo-Bot in action!
